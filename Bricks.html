BRICKS + MORTAR
(2019)


100 years after the Addison Act, a move that made individual housing needs a national responsibility and aimed to provide good quality housing for working people, and the state of our housing is not only a national crisis but a national scandal.

On November the 11th 2019, Bristol was reminded of the dire situation it finds itself in as the third year BA Hons Graphic students took to the streets to express our collective distain (with some great megagraphics)!

Myself and three others organised an exhibition at the Vestibules (Bristol City Hall) on November the 18th to showcase this work + have some great (and angry) discussions.

2021, Ella Staines
